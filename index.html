Rules

Ping Pong is a game that will count up to the number you enter starting from 1 with the following exceptions:

  • Numbers divisible by 3 will become 'PING'
  • Numbers divisible by 5 will become 'PONG'
  • Numbers divisible by 15 will become 'PINGPONG'
